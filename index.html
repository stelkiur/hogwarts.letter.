Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

Welcome, young witch or wizard!

Hogwarts is a magical school hidden deep within the Scottish Highlands. For centuries, it has trained young witches and wizards in spells, potions, and magical creatures. Students are sorted into four unique houses, fostering friendship, courage, wisdom, and ambition. The castle is alive with enchantments, hidden passages, and secret treasures, offering adventures unlike any other.
